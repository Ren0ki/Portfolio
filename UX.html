Project 1: What Can a Starship Do?


For this assignment, team was asked to come with a solution that improves human to human connection with the Purdue Starship system. My team's involves a sticker app called Starship: StickerScapes which is an app that gives users a chance to earn digital stickers when interacting with Starships. The app also implements a social media-based system that allows users to interact with others with personalized avatars. As a result, the school's community will have the opportunity to interact with peers over a shared leisurely activity.




Project 2: Embodied Shopping



Here, our team was tasked with finding a solution to encourage in-person shopping. Our design involved the implementation of a mobile app that guides users through the in-person shopping experience. Through survey-based research, we found that users would benefit from a product that bridges the gap between online and in-person shopping. Thus, the technological aspect of this app can give users a sense of familarity in an otherwise unexplored environment.
